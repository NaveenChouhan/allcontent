Education

school education

1984-DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir in Ranchi:

MS Dhoni's formal education includes completing high school from DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir in Ranchi, securing 66% and 56% in his high school and intermediate exams respectively.

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) degree:

1999 - St. Xavier's College in Ranchi

MS Dhoni pursued a B.Com degree from St. Xavier's College in Ranchi, but he dropped out to focus on his cricket career. He was admitted to the college in 1999 through the sports quota.

Prioritizing Cricket:

While at college, he prioritized cricket and ultimately dropped out to focus on his professional cricketing career.

Experience

Early Career and Rise to Prominence:

Dhoni's international debut was in 2004, playing for India against Bangladesh. He quickly established himself as a skilled batsman and wicketkeeper, making his Test debut in 2005.

Captaincy and Leadership:

Under Dhoni's captaincy, India achieved significant success in various formats of the game. He led the team to victory in the 2011 Cricket World Cup, a historic moment for Indian cricket.

Post-Captaincy Career:

Dhoni continued to play for India and Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He played a pivotal role in CSK's success, leading the team to multiple IPL titles.
